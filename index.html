Are you struggling or is your loved one suffering of addiction?

Providing advocacy, intensive and comprehensive care to people who are struggling with substance abuse and process/ behavioral addiction by using universally and accepted evidence – based practices.

Dr. ISACK: Qualifications:

  • Holds Degree of Doctor of Medicine from Muhimbili University of Health and Allied Sciences (MUHAS) – Tanzania.
  • Graduate of Colombo Plan ICCE Post-graduate Fellowship in Addiction Science at Cyberjaya University College of Medical Sciences (CUCMS) – Malaysia.
  • Holds International Certified Addiction Professional (ICAP-1),
  • Has more than five (5) years of experience in the field of Treatment of Substance Use Disorders.

The services provided are based on approved techniques such as Cognitive Behavioural Therapy, Motivational Interviewing in Individual and Family/Group Therapy.

DO YOU HAVE HEALTH CONCERN AND DON’T KNOW WHAT TO DO?

The aim of this program is to facilitate engagement of patients to the appropriate and cost-effective health and wellness care and reducing anxiety of facing barriers to proper services. The patient is helped to navigate the avenue of services and have the best options pertaining to the complexicity of the need.

DO YOU FACE A CHALLENGE AND NEED MORE INFORMATION ON PROPER USE OF PADS/DIAPERS?

The use of sanitary pads and baby diapers has been associated with ill-health to women of reproductive age as well as to babies such as skin itching and burning, rashes, foul smelling, pain and opportunistic infections to urinary and reproductive tracts. The ineffective and unhygienic ways of using the women pads and baby’s diapers is the underlying concern. Therefore, this program has been designed to provide FREE CONSULTATION to best ways of using pads/diapers to prevent, reduce and care the unhealthy consequences.

DO YOU NEED TO SHARPEN THE BEHAVIOUR OF YOU CHILD?

This program has been planned to facilitate the primary school pupils to have the desirable behaviors for significant academic achievement, morale and balanced life. Evidence-based psychosocial approaches have been incorporated to produce the expected outcomes.

DO YOU WANT TO SEE YOUR CHILD ACHIEVE HER/HIS DREAM?

The program is designed to facilitate secondary students to acquire necessary skills and knowledge to achieve academic excellent and choose the appropriate carriers. It is coupled with evidencebased techniques to help the student focus and have multiple skills towards successful life.

DO YOU WANT TO MAKE USEFUL OF YOUR EDUCATION AND BUILD MULTIPLE SOURCES OF INCOME?

This program is for college students and graduates. It has been designed to integrate the knowledge and skills acquired to create income. The best evidencebased approaches have been improvised to achieve the demand of individual person.

BORN RICH, RAISED POOR, MADE GREAT

Category: Personal development

Being born and raised to harsh conditions and adversities leaves many- victims and broken. Dreams are shut at tender age and life becomes a viscous cycle of hopelessness, pains and failure. This Keynote narrates a real story of how a person can heal through pains and change the story of his/her life; regains hope, dream again, make the dream reality and becomes a point of reference to those who aspire the bright future.

Audience: Youth, Adults, Parents/Guardians

CLASSES UNDER THE TREE

Category: Personal development
